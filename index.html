About Me

I am an Information Technology student at the Harare Institute of Technology where I am one of the university’s most recognised student voices, twice selected as a university representative on national platforms.

My work lives at the intersection of data, software, community and advocacy. I build solutions and tell stories that centre the people most often left out of the conversations.

Through my internship in Software Development at the Africa Clinical Research Network I am growing my understanding of software and contributing to building systems that actually help people.

“I don’t just want to work in tech. I want to ensure that when Africa tells its own story through data, that story is told with precision, with dignity and with power.”

I hold the McKinsey Forward Certificate and a Google Data Analytics Certificate, and I am currently completing additional professional courses to strengthen my technical and storytelling skills.
