About Me

I am an Information Technology student at the Harare Institute of Technology where I am one of the university's most recognised student voices, twice selected as a keynote speaker at TEDx and HIT graduation ceremonies.

My work lives at the intersection of data, software, community and advocacy. I build solutions and tell stories that centre the people most often left out of the conversation—creating technology that works for all.

Through my internship in Software Development at the Africa Clinical Research Network I am growing my understanding of software, contributing to building systems that support healthcare delivery across Africa.

"I don't just want to work in tech. I want to ensure that when Africa tells its own story through data, that story is told with precision, with dignity and with power."

I hold the McKinsey Forward Certificate, Google Data Analytics Certificate, and am pursuing additional credentials in emerging tech fields. I'm committed to continuous learning and using technology for social impact.
